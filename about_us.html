Our School Motto:

>>Believe in yourself!!

Ethos:

>>To instil a love of learning for life in all our pupils and to provide them with a safe, inclusive and nurturing learning environment that helps them grow personally and academically

Our Core School Values:

>>Our school values are Honesty, Independence, Collaboration, Respect and Courage.

Our Vision:

>>Our Vision is to be an exemplary institution by offering excellence in academics, sports, arts, technology and beyond

Our Mission:

>>To maintain a high quality, intellectually stimulating, open and realistic educational environment where every student is valued

Our Aims & Objectives:

  • To develop a learning community based on excellence and enjoyment.
  • To develop independent learners and critical thinkers with an enthusiasm for long life learning.
  • To foster in all our students respect for themselves and for the global community.
  • To promote tolerance and respect from people from different cultures.
  • To promote care and respect for the environment.
  • To develop compassionate individuals who are willing to contribute to their local and wider communities.

Our History:

The Manmms Kindergarten was established in January 2000 in Nairobi, at which time, there were roughly 50 students and 5 teachers.
since then, we have grown to around 200 students and 20 teachers.
Our Programs include day care & pre-primary. We offer a caring, nurturing and safe enviroment for children to learn. We aim to develop children with knowledge,skills, attitudes and character to ensure their success.
We provide your child with an enjoyable, stimulating and educational time and to teach them a variety of skills and social graces to help them follow on to their next big adventure - Prep school!
Our staff team is very experienced and committed to supporting the children and their families. It is important to us that your child is happy and secure and makes good progress in their time at our school.
In pursuant to our mission we will provide a safe, caring, friendly and secure learning enviroment for all our children.
We will endeavour to create a well guided and holistic individual who will be able to proceed with further learning in any school. Backing our mission we will provide relevant learning materials and books,teach technology, encourage creativity through art and teach social skills through practical life learning and outdoor activities.

Our Staff:

Our School employs a team of highly qualified and enthusiastic teachers dedicated to providing students with access to a broad curriculum and extensive co-curricular programme.
we are committed to safeguarding and promoting the welfare of your children.
All applicants to our School undergo child protection screening, including checks with past employers, criminal record, disclosure and barring service checks.

School Menu:

The school provides a healthy snack and a nutritious, well balanced lunch. We have a qualified chef who prepares a balanced diet which includes:

  • Vegetables with meals all days
  • Snacks for break include cereals.biscuits,cake taken with chocolate, juice or tea
  • Beef, chicken or fish with meals like rice, pasta, mashed potatoes, bananas, ugali and chapati
  • Fruits on a daily basis
  • Clean water is also provided.
